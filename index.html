About Me

Jimi Loc Nguyen hails from the Univeresity of Michigan where he studied Dancer Performance and Composition. After completing his BFA at the School of Music, Theatre, and Dance, he continued on to travel nationally and internationally during his performance career. After many years abroad, Jimi settled back in the Midwest, where he will forever call his homebase.

Today, Jimi works as a Chiropractic technician by day, and an overly enthusiastic coder by night at Northwestern University in Evanston. He is excited for his trajectory forward into the world of technology and computer sciences.
